## User Roles

### Aspirant STAR
**Purpose**: Individuals applying to become church volunteers
**Capabilities**:
- View personal journey timeline
- Track progress through 6 steps
- Update profile information
- Access training materials
- Receive notifications about progress

### Mentor
**Purpose**: Guide aspirants through ministry training
**Capabilities**:
- View assigned aspirants
- Track mentee progress
- Submit evaluation reports
- Access training resources
- Schedule training sessions

### MDS (Ministry of STAR)
**Purpose**: Conduct interviews and validate aspirants
**Capabilities**:
- Schedule and conduct interviews
- Approve or reject aspirants at Step 3
- View aspirant progress
- Generate interview reports

### Administrator
**Purpose**: Manage the entire STAR system
**Capabilities**:
- Manage all aspirants and users
- Validate step progressions
- Configure system settings
- Generate comprehensive reports
- Manage ministries and assignments

### Pastor
**Purpose**: Oversee the entire STAR program
**Capabilities**:
- View global dashboard with analytics
- Access program statistics
- Review final assignments
- Monitor overall program health

## The STAR Journey

### Step 1: Application
- **Duration**: 7 days
- **Process**: Complete online registration form
- **Outcome**: Become "Aspirant STAR"
- **Validation**: Administrator review

### Step 2: PCNC Training
- **Duration**: 6 months (180 days)
- **Process**: Complete Pastoral Care and Nurture Course
- **Requirements**: Attend sessions, complete assignments
- **Validation**: Administrator confirmation

### Step 3: MDS Interview
- **Duration**: 14 days
- **Process**: Interview with Ministry of STAR team
- **Outcome**: Approval or rejection for ministry training
- **Validation**: MDS decision

### Step 4: Ministry Training
- **Duration**: 30 days
- **Process**: Training within chosen ministry with mentor
- **Requirements**: Work closely with assigned mentor
- **Validation**: Mentor and administrator approval

### Step 5: Mentor Report
- **Duration**: 7 days
- **Process**: Mentor submits evaluation report
- **Outcome**: Favorable or unfavorable recommendation
- **Validation**: Administrator review

### Step 6: Confirmation & Assignment
- **Duration**: 7 days
- **Process**: Final assignment to ministry role
- **Outcome**: Active STAR volunteer status
- **Validation**: Pastor or administrator approval

3. **Generate Report**: Dashboard → Generate Reports

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Login Problems**:
- Verify email and password are correct
- Check if account is active
- Contact administrator if locked out

**Dashboard Not Loading**:
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try different browser
- Check internet connection

**Step Not Progressing**:
- Ensure all requirements are met
- Contact mentor or administrator
- Check for pending validations

**Notifications Not Received**:
- Check notification settings
- Verify email address is correct
- Check spam/junk folder

## Security Guidelines

**Password Security**:
- Use minimum 8 characters
- Include uppercase, lowercase, numbers
- Avoid common words or personal info
- Change passwords regularly

**Account Security**:
- Log out when finished
- Don't share login credentials
- Report suspicious activity
- Keep contact information current

**Data Privacy**:
- Access only necessary information
- Respect confidentiality of aspirant data
- Follow church privacy policies
- Report data breaches immediately
